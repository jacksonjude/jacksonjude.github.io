** Personal Data Usage **

The bus-tracking app "Bussr" will be reffered to as "the app" and users of the app will be reffered to as "the users" or "the user"

Favorite and Recent stops

For ease of use, favorite and recently viewed stops will be synced to the cloud via the user's iCloud account if possible. This information is within their private database, and CAN ONLY be accessed by the user via their iCloud credentials.
